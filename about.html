About SMCS

We built SMCS to solve one of the most overlooked challenges in military operations — the human cost of disconnection from family and the security risks of unsecured communications.

Our Mission

SMCS is a purpose-built secure communication platform designed around the realities of military deployment. We combine iron-clad security with resilience under the worst network conditions — ensuring no soldier is ever truly unreachable, and no message is ever lost.

Node.js Backend Socket.IO Real-time AES-256 / RSA-2048 Offline Message Queue JWT Authentication Dark / Light Mode
